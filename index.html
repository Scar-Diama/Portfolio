About Me

Games. That's the catalyst that sparked my passion for programming and software development.

Hi! I'm Shea, and from a young age, that's what most my free time was spent on. As the years went on, that passion evolved into understanding the inner workings of games, then eventually wanting to build my own. A friend showed me raw code and something clicked. I quit my electrical apprenticeship, went to college, then Northumbria to presue my goal of becoming a programmer!

Since then I've worked across multiple systems: low-level Assembly, C++ game systems, Unreal Engine, 2D engines, file parsing and more. I enjoy understanding how systems run at the deeper levels, never settling for superficial solutions. Code quality and understanding is paramount to not only me, but to the industry as a whole, as that can determine the difference between a good game and a great one.

My passion for coding doesn't just end at games, software development and engineering is also a big interest of mine which brings great satisfaction when I can solve a problem with an elegant solution. And because of my background in games and skillset, I can bring that knowledge to software roles.

I'm open to opportunities across the UK and Western Europe.
